GridWatch

A system for detecting and predicting power outages around the world.

GridWatch uses weather data, infrastructure risk, and expert analysis to predict and respond to grid failures faster. To work with us to help train our models, please visit the careers tab above.

Example view:

What you're seeing:

Each circle represents the risk of an outage occuring in an area. For example, the bright green circle at Charlottesville represents a very low (~0%-10%) chance of a power outage occuring there. The orange and red circles near Virginia Beach represent a much higher (~50%+) risk of outages near that area.
