About GridWatch

This website was started during HackTJ, the nation's largest high school hackathon, where it won the Terac challenge. However, since the hackathon, many significant changes have been made, with the biggest increase in functionality being the integration of the Geocodio API and an autofill function allowing users to choose any city or zip code within the US and get a probability of a power outage. This also works for email alerts. The theme for HackTJ was "Invisible Infrastructure". Our team members are below.
